## Why this contract exists

The DBA `--live --confirm` run passed contract → ingest → gate1 → power, then halted at **prep** with `declared column resolves to more than one export column (FR-103/104)`: demographic `D3` (`column_hint: D3`) matched both `Q44` and `Q44_5_TEXT`. Read-only inspection of the raw Qualtrics export resolved the full demographic picture:

| Demographic | Question (row-1 text) | Selected-choice col | Text sidecar |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | gender | Q42 | — |
| D2 | age group | Q43 | — |
| D3 | education level | **Q44** | Q44_5_TEXT |
| D4 | nationality | **Q45** | Q45_2_TEXT |
| D5 | department | **Q46** | Q46_7_TEXT |
| D6 | monthly income (QAR) | Q47 | — |
| D7 | industry | **Q48** | Q48_9_TEXT |
| D8 | company size | Q49 | — |
| D9 | AI awareness | Q50 | — |
| D10 | *(does not exist — survey skips D9→D11)* | — | — |
| D11 | AI tool usage frequency | Q51 | — |
| D12 | formal AI training | Q52 | — |
| D13 | interest in AI training | Q53 | — |

Two distinct problems:
1. **Selected-choice + text-sidecar ambiguity** (D3, D5, D7 — and D4 which has the same pattern): a demographic with an "Other (please specify)" option produces two columns in Qualtrics — the choice column and a `..._N_TEXT` sidecar whose row-1 question text embeds the **same** demographic label. The crosswalk's `_account_roles.resolve()` correctly halts on the >1 match (FR-103/104). The engine must resolve the demographic to the **selected-choice** column and account for the `_TEXT` sidecar as an **ignored** column — not treat it as a competing demographic match.
2. **Source-contract errors for D4 and D10:**
   - **D4 (nationality):** the question row-1 text is "What is your nationality?" with **no** "D4" label embedded, so `column_hint: D4` never matches its column (Q45). Its hint must bind to the nationality question.
   - **D10:** the survey has **no D10** (Q50 = D9, Q51 = D11); it was removed in survey design and never reindexed. The phantom `D10` entry must be removed from the source's `data.demographics`.

## Researcher decisions (recorded, authoritative)

1. **Selected-choice binding:** each affected demographic binds to its selected-choice column (D3→Q44, D4→Q45, D5→Q46, D7→Q48). **Confirmed.**
2. **Text sidecars ignored:** `Q44_5_TEXT`, `Q45_2_TEXT`, `Q46_7_TEXT`, `Q48_9_TEXT` are "Other-specify" free-text columns, **not analyzed** in the CB-SEM; they are accounted for as ignored columns. **Confirmed.**
3. **D4 nationality:** kept as a descriptive demographic (sample characteristic, not in the structural model), bound to its nationality question column (Q45). **Confirmed.**
4. **D10:** removed from `data.demographics` — it does not exist in the instrument/export. **Confirmed.**

## Objective & Scope

Resolve every demographic (and its text sidecar) to exactly one role so prep's FR-103/104 / zero-orphan accounting passes, without weakening the ambiguity/orphan guards, and correct the D4/D10 source-contract facts.

**In scope:**
- **Crosswalk demographic resolution:** when a declared demographic resolves to a Qualtrics selected-choice column **and** its paired `..._TEXT` sidecar, bind the demographic to the selected-choice column and account for the `..._TEXT` sidecar as an ignored/other-specify column. A demographic resolving to two **non-sidecar** columns must still halt (real ambiguity preserved). Detection of the sidecar relationship must be deterministic (e.g. the `..._N_TEXT` naming paired to the same base question / the row-1 "Other (please specify) - Text" marker).
- **Source correction (D4/D10):** correct the study-source so Node A emits the right demographic contract — D4 nationality binds to its question column, and D10 is removed. Because the source document (`study_document.docx` = the specification) drives Node A, this correction is made **in the specification / study source**, then re-extracted through the governed live path (the config is not hand-edited — that would break the glance token, per prior ruling). If the demographic column bindings are better expressed as explicit per-demographic `column_hint` values the crosswalk consumes, that mechanism must be implemented and tested here; otherwise the selected-choice/sidecar resolution above handles D3/D5/D7 and the spec correction handles D4/D10.
- Tests using a **fixture** mirroring the real Qualtrics demographic structure (selected-choice + `_TEXT` sidecar pairs; a nationality-style unlabeled question; a skipped-number case).

**Explicitly out of scope:**
- No change to the 11-construct model, the 45 modeled items, the two second-order structures, or the 9 hypotheses — all validated and untouched.
- No weakening of FR-103/104 real-ambiguity halt or V6 zero-orphan (a genuinely ambiguous or genuinely orphaned column must still halt).
- No change to statistical modules, certification, `renv.lock`, or other governed docs beyond this contract + index.
- No hand-edit of the DBA `study_config.yaml` or the raw CSV.
